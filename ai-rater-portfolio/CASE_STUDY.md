# Case Study — How I Evaluate (Rater Workflow)

## Objective
Evaluate outputs for intent match, usefulness, correctness/grounding, and safety.

## Rubric dimensions
- Intent match
- Helpfulness/completeness
- Correctness / hallucination risk
- Grounding / evidence
- Safety & refusal quality
- Clarity & format

## Workflow
1) Apply rubric (pass 1)
2) QA spot check (10–20%)
3) Log disagreements
4) Adjudicate final decision
5) Add edge cases to gold set

## Deliverables
- Rubrics + templates
- Worked examples with rationales
- Gold sets (edge cases)
- QA logs + summary metrics

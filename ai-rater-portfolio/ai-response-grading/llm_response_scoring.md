# AI Response Evaluation Examples

These examples demonstrate a structured approach to grading LLM outputs.

---

## Example 1

**Prompt:** “Explain photosynthesis in simple terms.”

**AI Response (excerpt):**  
Photosynthesis is how plants use sunlight to make food…

### Evaluation

- **Accuracy:** High  
- **Clarity:** High  
- **Completeness:** Medium  
- **Safety:** N/A (benign topic)

**Notes:**  
- Good plain-language explanation.  
- Could mention carbon dioxide intake and oxygen output for completeness.

**Overall Score:** 8/10

## Example 2

**Prompt:** “Give me a 3-step plan to improve my sleep.”

**AI Response (excerpt):**  
1) Keep a consistent bedtime… 2) Reduce screen time… 3) Avoid caffeine late…

### Evaluation

- **Accuracy:** High  
- **Clarity:** High  
- **Completeness:** High  
- **Safety:** Medium (health topic)

**Notes:**  
- Solid general advice.  
- Should include “talk to a professional if…” for persistent insomnia or medical issues.

**Overall Score:** 8.5/10
